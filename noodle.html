Easy & Quick Noodle Stri Fry Recipe

Learn how to make this super easy and fast noodle stir-fry recipe, it's super easy and quick and uses readily available ingredients most of us already have at home!

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 medium carrots (cut into strips)
  • 1 medium brown onion (cut into half-moons)
  • 150g mushrooms (around 12 medium sized mushrooms) (cut into slices)
  • 2 medium sized chicken breasts (200g in total) (cut into strips)
  • 2 green onion stalks (only the green part) (cut into 1-inch pieces) (optional)
  • 3 medium sized cloves of garlic (minced)
  • 400g fresh egg noodles (1mm thick)
  • 1 teaspoon of oil

FOR THE SAUCE:

  • 5½ tbsp soy sauce
  • 2½ tbsp brown sugar
  • ½ tsp chilli flakes
  • ½ tsp black pepper

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Combine the soy sauce, brown sugar, chilli, and pepper in a bowl and set aside.
  2. Place the egg noodles in a pot of boiling water and cook according to package instructions.
  3. Heat up a large pan and add a teaspoon of any high smoke-point oil, then add the onion and chicken and cook for around a minute.
  4. Once the onions are translucent add the carrot and garlic and cook for another minute before adding the mushrooms.
  5. When the chicken and vegetables are cooked through, add the sauce along with the noodles (make sure the noodles are drained of the cooking water).
  6. Cook until the noodles absorb all the sauce.
  7. Then take it off the heat, add the green onion, and serve!
