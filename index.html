A Pinch of Salt

About

A pinch of salt is a collection of my favorite recipes, however I know the pain of wanting to make a certain recipe but being constrained by the ingredients you have available. Hence, the website will allow you to input the ingeredients you have available and it will suggest recipes that utilise those ingredients.

Recipes

In the website, you can find a vast collection of savory and sweet recipes suited for all cooking levels, ranging all the way from éclairs to ramen. You can also select the ingredients you have available and in turn the website will suggest reciped using those ingredients. You can access all the recipes from the "Recipes" tab.
