Quick Breakfast Toast Recipe

Learn how to make this super easy breakfast egg toast recipe, it's one of my favorite breakfast recipes as its quick, healthy, and delicious!

INGREDIENTS

  • 2 eggs
  • 100g smoked salmon
  • 1 tbsp capers
  • ½ avacado
  • 2oz cream cheese
  • ½ lemon
  • 1 sourdough bread slice
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • sesame seeds
  • 3 tbsp cream

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. by making the marinadeing the chicken, combine the mustard, orange juice, honey, vinegar, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  2. Add the chicken breasts into the marinade, and let them marinade for atleast 30 minutes.
  3. Using a vegetable peeler, slice the cucumber into thin ribbons.
  4. Cut the onion into half moons and slice the strawberries into thin slices.
  5. To make the dressing, combine the ornage juice, mustard, honey, and salt in a bowl. Then emulsify the mixture by addding the olive oil in a low steady stream whisking all the while.
  6. Heat up a indoor or outdoor grill, and place the chicken onto it adding half of the marinade. Allow the chicken to cook for around 5-7 minutes each side or until the internal temperature reaches 165F (74C).
  7. Once the chicken is fully cooked take it off the grill, and let it rest while you prepare the rest of the salad.
  8. In a bowl, toss together the arugula, cucumber ribbons, sliced strawberries, onion half moons, as well as the dressing.
  9. Slice the chicken and toss in with the rest of the salad and serve!
